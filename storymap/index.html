Swiss Monopoly Tour

After playing Monopoly with my kids (5, 7, 9) for years, we decided to visit these places once in reality. The challenge was to travel to all places by public transport within two days.

Unlike the original and most other editions, the Swiss Monopoly edition has existing streets and places in Swiss cities. The following map shows our route from January 3rd and 4th 2018.

Start at 6:27 in Stargans
